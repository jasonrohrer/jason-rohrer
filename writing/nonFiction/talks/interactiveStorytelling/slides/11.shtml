David Jaffe on his failed Heartland project:

"The minute I give you a gasoline can, or the minute I say go up after the teenage son who's running to his room, [you think] 'Oh maybe I'll go up here. Oh what's in this room over here?' or 'Oh, hey look at the mother down there on her knees. What if I shoot her? Oh, well I can't shoot her, that will fuck up the story.'"

"So many things come into play with that whole playground mentality that all of that carefully plotted, carefully intended thematic and storytelling, it just crumbles."
