Near Mama's Heart My Baby and More - Because Kids don't come with Instructions! Specializing in Parent Coaching and Education, Breastfeeding and Babywearing Support. Home of Near Mama's Heart, by Colleen Newman, a children's book about breastfeeding. www.mybabyandmore.com

The Wonder's of Mother's Milk Have you ever had to explain why a mother would choose to breastfeed? Has explaining the natural process of breastfeeding to a child been challenging? The answer to these questions, and many more are now found in the new children's book, The Wonders of Mother's Milk. www.breast-feedingamerica.com/books/index.php

I'm Made of Mama's Milk A children's board book which portrays the precious breastfeeding relationship between mother and child. www.maryolsenbooks.org/index.htm

LLL's list of children's books showing breastfeeding Children's books tend to reflect our society's bias towards feeding babies by bottle. Here are some of the better books currently available that show or tell about breastfeeding, arranged in order from those appropriate for the youngest children to those intended for older readers. Look for them in your library or bookstore. Illustrations are by the author where not otherwise indicated. Remember that no book is perfect or will fit every need. www.lalecheleague.org/cbi/bfbookshort.htm
