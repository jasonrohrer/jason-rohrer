Saturday with Mez:
A Day in the Life of a Breastfeeding Toddler



A children's board book portraying a breastfeeding toddler during one of his typical days. The book features 28 pages of high quality black and white photos. It is both printed and bound in the U.S.A. Current and former breastfed children of all ages now have a story they can relate to.
