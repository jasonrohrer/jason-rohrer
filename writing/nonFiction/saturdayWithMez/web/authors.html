Lauren Serafin, Jason Rohrer, Mez and Ayza live in Las Cruces, NM. There they live a life of voluntary simplicity. It took a little planning, but they were able to cut out many of the material needs that they once depended on in order to be able to spend the time they wanted to on their own projects as well as with each other. Instead of working traditional jobs, they are free to seek out interesting projects that sometimes help them make ends meet. They also have free time to do local volunteer work as well.
